Sports Clubhouse

A personally built app that allows users to follow their favorite sports teams and customize a news feed, consolidating all their favorite news sources in one place. Users can also view a team schedule that pulls minimum ticket prices from Ticketmaster's API, and set a price alert that will send users a text through Twilio when the price has been met. This application uses Ruby on Rails, Javascript, and VueJS.
